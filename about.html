About Me.

Oscar Puig Canal is a Barcelona-based illustrator and artist specializing in bold, high-energy artwork for entertainment, licensed products, music, apparel, and publishing. His style combines vibrant color, detailed linework, dynamic composition, and a strong pop-culture sensibility influenced by comics, animation, punk rock, skate graphics, fantasy art, and underground music culture.

He has created artwork for international brands, studios, and commercial projects across guidebook illustration, promotional products, apparel, posters, album covers, packaging, logos, and key art. With a background in illustration and graphic design, Oscar brings both expressive image-making and production-aware thinking to each project.

His work is especially suited to fantasy, horror, gaming, music, youth culture, and character-driven worlds, making him a strong collaborator for studios developing artwork for licensed IP, merchandise, publishing, collectibles, and entertainment campaigns.

Oscar also runs Felina Project , a clothing brand and creative initiative supporting environmental and animal welfare causes.
